About Us

Trees of Life Pasting Community Centre is a registered non-profit organisation committed to supporting vulnerable children, strengthening families and empowering communities through quality programmes and partnerships.

Our Vision

To build safe, caring communities where every child has the opportunity to learn, grow and thrive.

Our Mission

To provide Early Childhood Development, child protection, youth development and family support services that improve lives and create lasting change.

Our Programmes

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD)
  • Orphans and Vulnerable Children Support
  • Youth Development
  • Family Support
  • Community Outreach

Community Impact

Our volunteers work alongside community members to provide education, support services, outreach programmes and opportunities that strengthen vulnerable families.
