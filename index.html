About Us

Trees of Life Pasting Community Centre is a registered non-profit organisation committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children, families, and communities. We provide support through Early Childhood Development, child protection, family support, youth development, and community outreach programmes.

Our Vision

To create safe, nurturing communities where children and families have opportunities to learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Mission

To protect vulnerable children, strengthen families, and empower communities through quality programmes, partnerships, and compassionate support.

Our Programmes

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD)
    Providing safe learning opportunities and early support to young children.
  • Orphans and Vulnerable Children Support
    Supporting vulnerable children and families through care, referrals, and community assistance.
  • Youth Development
    Creating opportunities for youth empowerment, skills development, and personal growth.
  • Family Support
    Strengthening families through outreach, guidance, and community support.

Our Community Impact

Trees of Life currently supports community members through outreach, home visits, volunteer programmes, and assistance to vulnerable families.

Together with our volunteers and partners, we work towards creating lasting positive change.

Partner With Us
